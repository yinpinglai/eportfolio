Collaborative Discussion 2 - Summary Post


I demonstrated my scanning results in the initial post. I can see that most of us were getting very similar results even though we were scanning for different websites. Also, I can see a variety of tools have been used to carry out this scanning exercise, such as TraceRoute, NsLookup, dig, whois, etc. I believe that most of us can get familiar with these tools throughout this exercise and use it in our final assignment.

In addition, I observed that some of my classmates were confused about calculating the average duration of latency. (Hung L. 2022.) figured out that the term "latency" somehow refers to the "delay" while reading the TraceRoute result. So, the average duration of latency can be calculated by measuring the delay between each hop.

Lastly, since our assigned websites are shared hosting, the website will block our IP addresses after conducting a few scans. (Thien L. 2022.) came up with a solution that we can bypass the security gateway to get rid of this problem.
