Collaborative Discussion 2 - Scanning Exercise and Results Week 1


The following tools have been used for carry out the scanning exercise for our assigned blog site: allmytype.co.uk

  1. TraceRoute
  2. NSLookup
  3. WHOIS

I applied above tools to answer below questions of unit 3 of this module and questions and answers are listed below.

How many hops from your machine to your assigned website?

I used the TraceRoute tool to count hops to reach to the assigned blog site. It took 15 hops to reach out there.

Which step causes the biggest delay in the route? What is the average duration of that delay?

The biggest delay was the network communication between telia.net and the average duration was 300ms.

What are the main nameservers for the website?

I used the NSLookup tool to find out the main nameservers for the assigned blog site. The results are listed below.

  1. ns1.a2hosting.com
  2. ns2.a2hosting.com
  3. ns3.a2hosting.com
  4. ns4.a2hosting.com
Who is the registered contact?

I used the WHOIS tool to find out hte registration information for the assigned blog site. The site's registration information are listed below.

  • Registrar:
    eNom LLC [Tag = ENOM]
    URL: http://www.enom.com
What is the MX record for the website?

I used the NSLookup tool to find out the MX record for the assigned blog site and found the following record.

  • MX preference = 0
    mail exchanger = mail.allmytype.co.uk
Where is the website hosted?

As I couldn't perform pinging to the assigned blog site, so I used the "whatismyipaddress.com" to obtain the IP address for the assigned blog site and then obtain the location info for the IP address. The website is hosted in Amsterdam, the Netherland.
