Seminar 5 - Data Breach Case Study


What types of data were affected?

PSN users’ personal data such as names, addresses, login details, and security questions and 77 millions of users’ credit card data.

What happened?

Sony’s team noticed an unauthorized intrusion occurred and above data were transmitted without authorization.

Who was responsible?

Sony

Were any escalation(s) stopped - how?

Not applicable

Was the Business Continuity Plan instigated?

Not applicable

Was the ICO notified?

Yes. Sony reported the incident to ICO.

Were affected individuals notified?

Yes. Sony warned those affected individuals after one week.

What were the social, legal and ethical implications of the decisions made?
  • Social implication: Sony failed to take immediate action warn affected individuals after incident happened.
  • Legal implication: Sony was fined £250,000 ($395k) by ICO on violating the UK’s Data Protection Act 1998 for this data breach incident.
  • Ethical implication: Sony held accountability for protecting users’ information.
