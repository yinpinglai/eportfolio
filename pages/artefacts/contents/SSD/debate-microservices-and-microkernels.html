Debate - MicroServices and MicroKernels are the future?


Torvalds has been proven wrong and it only took nearly thirty years. Microservices and microkernels are the future.

I agreed with above statement. I believed that Microservices and Microkernels are the future. In the recent decade, the market trends are changing rapidly. Companies must take an action to respond to the market swiftly. Consequently, they are adopting Microservices and Microkernels to catch the market trend.

The term microservices is a design of software architecture which contains different processes to fulfil business needs (microservices.io. N.D.). Below are pros and cons for microservices:

Pros:

  • Easy to maintain and test
  • Loosely coupled
  • Flexibility of using different technical stacks

Cons:

  • Communication overhead
  • Consume more resources
  • Hard for debugging and testing

The term microkernels is a containerized technology which embedded least components to let an operation system functioning (Techopedia, N.D.). Below are pros and cons for microkernels.

Pros

  • Secure environment
  • Easy for expansion
  • Isolated environment
  • Flexible to scale out

Cons:

  • Poor inter-process communication
  • Capable to handle small tasks
