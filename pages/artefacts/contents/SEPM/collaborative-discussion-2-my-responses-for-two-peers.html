While reading your post, I agreed that emotions are affecting humans on every single move and decision in our daily life. Humans are most likely change their mind and thoughts at different phases. (Adhim J. K., Pantjawati S. & Antok S. 2022) elicited that different variables impact the user experience and the way of using the service. It meant that the user experience can be affected by the service/product’s quality, accessibility, and content etc.

However, humans might make different decision based on their persona, such as the user’s age, gender, education background etc. Also, a widely studies in measuring user experience showed that the result can be distinct from various age group and gender. (Ahsanullah, Suziah S., Ahmad K. B. M., and Muzafar K. 2015).
