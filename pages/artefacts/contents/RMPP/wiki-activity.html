Find out if you need a visa to travel to Canada

The Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) is the department dedicated for dealing with immigartion, refugees, and Canadian Citizenship business. The purpose for this questionnaire is accessing the entry requirements and application details swiftly.

As one of the government departments, I believe that the questionnaire should be well-designed, easy to understand and appropriate language used for all kinds of travelers.

Questionnaire Layout & Design

The questionnaire is online accessible, and contains one question per page. All of the questions are designed in the closed questions way.

Pros
  • An indicator has shown above every question. So, the participant can get an insight about the length of remaining questions.
  • The font size, color, and space margin are appropriately and easy to read. The question has been bolded and eye-catching. So, it is intuitive for participants to focus on the questionnaire.
  • There is a navigator on the top left. The participant can go back to the previous and change their answers. So, the participant doesn’t have to start over the entire questionnaire from the beginning.
Cons
  • There is no glossary to check. The participant could not know the meaning of a specific term. For example, the meaning of the stateless persons might need to be further clarified.


Questionnaire Content

Overall all closed questions are standardized and non ambiguous questions presented. The questionnaire data can be easily converted for analyzing and giving the result to the participant.

Pros
  • Questions are focused on the aims of the questionnaire. No unnecessary questions/items presented.
  • The length of the questionnaire is short, logical and easy to follow.
  • Questions progress logically from the more general to specific.
