Creating an Object-Oriented Design for Supermarket


The Class Diagram Design of the Supermarket


I assume the supermarket is an online supermarket. Customers have to register their accounts for purchasing any goods from the supermarket.
Customers can add, remove products into/from their shopping cart. They also are allowed to add the product to their wish list. After the order has been delivered to customers successfully, the loyalty service will calculate the earned points for that order. It adds them to the customer's membership account.
The product details show the discount of the product when using points to check out.
